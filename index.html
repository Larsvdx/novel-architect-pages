Local-first by design

Your manuscript lives on your machine.

Novel Architect stores books, chapters, versions, and context notes in a local database. You can keep organizing and writing when the backend is unavailable. Online features are there when you need them, not as a requirement for every writing session.

AI workflows

Assistance for the parts of writing that benefit from another pass.

01

Writing assistance

Generate a chapter draft from your book details, selected context, previous chapters, skeleton, and current draft notes.

02

Revision assistance

Ask for a revision while keeping the result as a separate chapter version, so you can compare instead of overwriting your work.

03

The reviewer

Request editorial feedback across selected chapters and save the report beside the book for later review.

Context library

Give the AI better material, and give yourself a cleaner reference desk.

Store reusable story knowledge as structured entries for characters, locations, history, power systems, technology, factions, themes, and other notes. Active context can be selected for AI requests, while the same library helps you keep continuity in front of you while drafting.
